As the Layer 1 of Nervos Network, Common Knowledge Base (CKB) relies on Proof-of-Work (PoW) consensus for mining. Miners play a crucial role in ensuring security and decentralization within the ecosystem by validating transactions.

In exchange for their service, miners receive rewards in CKBytes. While mining can be rewarding and enjoyable, it comes with inherent risks and challenges. It's essential to understand these before diving in.
